Fun Fact #1

During the toddler years (ages 1 to 3), a child's brain develops more than at any other time in life. Their brain forms new neural connections at an astonishing rate—crucial for learning, language, and growth.

Fun Fact #2

Toddlers love negotiating—especially when it comes to clothing choices. Don’t be surprised if a superhero cape ends up in the grocery store aisle!

Fun Fact #3

Their emotional world is just beginning to unfold. Tantrums often happen because toddlers feel intensely—but can’t yet express themselves clearly.

Fun Fact #4

Mispronounced words can be both adorable and hilarious! From calling bananas "nanas" to cereal as "see-wee-ow," toddlers have their own fun language.

Fun Fact #5

Curiosity drives toddlers to explore everything. This sense of adventure is how they learn and connect with the world—through hands-on experiences and play.
